MiniTime — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025 · Operator: Stannum · Contact: contact@stannum.app

1. General

Stannum (“the Company”) respects your privacy and complies with applicable privacy laws. This policy applies to the use of the “MiniTime” mobile application (“the App”).

2. Information We Collect

  • The App does not collect any personal information.
  • Timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.
  • No account creation, server communication, or third-party analytics/advertising SDKs are used.

3. Purpose and Retention

  • Locally stored data is used solely to maintain App functionality and is never transmitted to a server.
  • All locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the App or manually reset its data.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The Company does not share, sell, or entrust personal information to any third parties.

5. User Rights

Because no personal data is stored on servers, there are no records subject to access, correction, or deletion requests. You can delete the App or reset data from your device settings at any time.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect data from children. If you have any concerns about child privacy, please contact us at the address below.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, no network transmission occurs. Users are encouraged to maintain device-level security through OS updates and lock settings.

8. International Transfers

No international transfer of personal data takes place.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated in accordance with legal or service changes. Any revisions will be communicated through the App or the official website.
