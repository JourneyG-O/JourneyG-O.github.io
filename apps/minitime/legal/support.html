Quick Help / FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?

  1. Open MiniTime → Settings.
  2. Tap Restore Purchases and sign in with the Apple ID you used to buy MiniTime Pro.
  3. After a moment, Pro should be re-enabled. If you still don’t see Pro, force-quit and reopen the app.

Note: Restores work only with the same Apple ID that originally purchased MiniTime Pro.

I see only up to 3 timers. Is that normal?

Yes. The free version lets you create up to three timers. MiniTime Pro unlocks unlimited timers and additional options.

I can’t hear the alert sound.

  • Check your device volume and Silent Mode.
  • In MiniTime, make sure Mute is off for the timer.
  • Confirm Focus or Do Not Disturb isn’t silencing notifications.

App crashes or doesn’t respond.

  • Update to the latest iOS and the latest MiniTime version.
  • Reboot your device.
  • If the issue persists, email us with steps to reproduce and your device/iOS version.
